Our AI team works on RAG pipelines, web scraping, LLM fine-tuning, agentic AI, FastAPI services, and Docker deployment.

RAG means Retrieval-Augmented Generation. In RAG, documents are split into chunks, converted into embeddings, stored in a vector database, and retrieved during question answering.

FastAPI is used to serve AI models through API endpoints. Docker is used to package and deploy the service.

LangChain helps connect LLMs, prompts, retrievers, vector databases, and document loaders.
